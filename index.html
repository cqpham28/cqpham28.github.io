Cuong Pham

Hi! I am currently a 1st-year PhD student at VinUni-Illinois Smart Healthcare Center (VinUniversity). Before that, I finished my Master degree in Information Science and Engineering at Ritsumeikan University. Previously, I worked as ML Engineer for a Danish startup HATO Medical Technologies, and Research Assitant at Brain Health Lab (VNU-HCM HCMIU).

My current research focus on digital phenotype monitoring, especially for neurological diseases and mental health illness, by leveraging multi-modalities data mining, bio-signal processing and artifical intelligent.
