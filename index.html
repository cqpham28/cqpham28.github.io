Research Interests

My current study focus on developing efficient multimodal AI framework for digital phenotype monitoring and assessment of some common neurological and mental health diseases, with a target on low-resource deployment setting.

I'm also interested in human-based biosignal experiments and analysis associated to different sub-domain studies in neurology, cardiology, and digital remote monitoring.
